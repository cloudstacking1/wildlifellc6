Frequently Asked Questions

What are the signs that I have a rat or wildlife problem in my home?

Common signs include scratching or scurrying sounds from your attic, walls, or crawl spaces — especially at night. You may also notice droppings, gnaw marks on wires or wood, greasy rub marks along baseboards, or an unexplained foul odor, which can indicate a dead animal hidden within your structure. Roof rats are especially prevalent in Central Florida and commonly enter through damaged roof vents, gaps in the roofline, or overhanging tree branches. If you notice any of these signs, contact Proteck Wildlife Solutions immediately for a thorough inspection.
